🎟️ PogoFestTicketsDiscordBot
The ultimate notification tool for Pokémon GO Fest 2026.

Never miss a ticket drop again. PogoFestTicketsDiscordBot monitors official Niantic and Pokémon GO event pages to alert your community the second tickets for Chicago, Tokyo, Copenhagen, or Global events go live.

### Key Features
Real-time Monitoring: Scans official sources every few minutes for updates.

Instant Alerts: Sends a @everyone or custom role ping in your designated channel.

Direct Links: Includes the official purchase URL to save you time navigating menus.

Clean Interface: Simple slash commands to set up and test notifications.

### How It Works
The bot uses a "watch-only" script to check the status of the Pokémon GO Live events page. It looks for specific changes in the HTML (like a "Sold Out" button turning into a "Buy Now" button) and triggers a message to your Discord server immediately.

⚖️ Legal & Compliance Note
This bot is designed for informational purposes only.

No Auto-Buying: This bot does not add tickets to carts or bypass checkout queues. It does not collect user payment information or login credentials.

Fair Play: We advocate for the Pokémon GO community to have a fair chance at tickets. This bot is a tool to help fans get to the site quickly, not to scalp or hoard tickets.

Terms of Service: This bot complies with Discord’s Developer Terms of Service and respects the robots.txt guidelines of the monitored sites.

### Quick Setup
Invite the bot to your server.

Use /set-channel to pick where alerts should go.

Use /ping-role to select which group should be notified.

Sit back and wait for the "Tickets are Live!" message!
